South Korea
I was born in a beautiful city, Busan, South Korea, where there's an ocean, a long mountain ranges, and man-built city buildings.
Nearly all of my childhood years were spent there, but frankly, I don't remember too much about my homeland.
I guess it's because I don't really have anyone to share those memories with.
Well, I think it's time to go visit again, to witness how my home country has changed in the past 10 years.
